About

I’m a self-taught front-end developer with a strong design sensibility and a passion for UX. I can help with any and every step of the website development process, including:

  • selecting domain and hosting services
  • designing information architecture and user experiences
  • building websites (using Wordpress, Squarespace, or HTML/CSS/JS)
  • graphic design and media asset creation
  • writing engaging copy and optimizing SEO
  • planning for future updates and maintenance

Whether you’re looking to redesign an existing website or build something from scratch, I’m happy to work with you to find a solution that meets your needs, budget, and technical skills. If you have questions or would like to work together, please send me an email.
