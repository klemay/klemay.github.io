Key Card name Meanings
0 The Fool beginnings, innocence, clear conscience

V The Hierophant education, belief systems, conformity, identification with a group

VII The Chariot victory, will, self-assertion, hard control, jurisdiction
VIII Strength fortitude, patience, compassion, security, soft control
IX The Hermit introspection, searching, guidance, solitude, wisdom
X The Wheel of Fortune destiny, turning point, movement, personal vision, global view
XI Justice equity, fairness, responsibility, decision, cause and effect
XII The Hanged Man letting go, reversal, suspension, sacrifice, grace
XIII Death ending, transition, inexorable forces, suffering, elimination
XIV Temperance balance, health, combination of forces
XV The Devil bondage, materialism, ignorance, hopelessness
XVI The Tower sudden change, release, downfall, upheaval, ego, hubris
XVII The Star hope, inspiration, generosity, sincerity
XVIII The Moon fear, illusion, anxiety, imagination, bewilderment, reflection
XIX The Sun enlightenment, greatness, vitality, assurance, glory
XX Judgement rebirth, inner calling, absolution, forgiveness, epiphany
XXI The World accomplishment, fulfillment, completion, consummation
